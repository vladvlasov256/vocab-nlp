Peter Drury started working for Sky Sports in the summer of 2023. He took the place of Martin Tyler as the commentator for the Premier League. Martin Tyler had worked at Sky Sports for about 30 years before this change. Many people know Martin Tyler as a very experienced football commentator. Sky Sports decided to choose Peter Drury to bring new ideas and a new voice. Martin Tyler said he understands why the company made the change. He feels happy about the decision, even if it is the end of a long time at Sky. Martin said it is important for companies to move forward and try new things. He will still work in football but in other ways. Fans remember his work with respect and admiration.
