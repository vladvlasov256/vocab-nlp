The Champions League is a big football event. It is for the best teams in Europe. This week, the last 16 teams play the second part of their games. These games are very important. Four teams from England play: Arsenal, Manchester City, Liverpool, and Chelsea. The games happen on Tuesday and Wednesday. The first games showed many goals. In total, 32 goals were scored in eight matches. The next games decide which teams go to the next round. The final will be in Budapest in 2026. This place is called the Puskas Arena.
