An American company called StoneX wants to buy CAB Payments. StoneX has offered £241.4 million for CAB Payments. This money is for all the shares of CAB. Each share is worth 95 pence. CAB Payments works in cross-border payments, which means moving money between countries. CAB has had other offers before, but those were not good enough. StoneX’s offer might make other companies want to buy CAB too. This can start a bidding war, where companies try to offer more money. The deal could change the future of CAB Payments quickly.
