A man from Deganwy in Conwy spends 44 hours on a hospital corridor. He is at Ysbyty Glan Clwyd hospital. He waits for a gallbladder operation. The operation is because his gallbladder is sick. It is gangrenous, so it must be removed. He feels very scared and thinks he might die. Many patients are treated in places like corridors in Betsi Cadwaladr hospitals.
