Elsevier is a big company that helps in science and healthcare with information. The Irish Research eLibrary is a group of libraries in Ireland that work together. They have started a new agreement for three years. This agreement helps researchers in Ireland read many journals. It also helps researchers publish their work without paying extra money. This is called open access publishing. It means everyone can read the research for free. The agreement is important for Irish universities and scientists. They can use important information for their studies. This helps science and health work grow in Ireland.
